About Hashtrend

It retrieves photos from the major social medias by their hashtags and creates an user-friendly and completely customizable social wall with the collected results

Hashtrend is designed for people who work in an artistic and / or design context and who need to effectively manage their web projects.

Thanks to Hashtrend it's easy to collect photos from the major social networks, save them or simply copy and paste the associated html code. It can be very useful to :

  • graphic designers
  • photographers
  • webmasters
  • web bloggers
  • ... and many others

Let's take a look at the Hashtrend's features

No more photos scattered around the web.

No more waste of time.

Make your project cleaner, work faster and in a smarter way.

Your time is precious, optimize it thanks to Hashtrend's efficient search algorithm !

Search for your wanted photos

Photographic research is really intuitive: just write a keyword in the search form and Hashtrend will collect all photos based on that specific hashtag to create an interactive photo wall. Hashtrend gets data through Instagram, Pinterest and Flickr APIs communication in order to guarantee the best result.

Manage your results

Copy and paste the associated HTML code of your wanted images to export them in your external projects or sign up / login to save your favourite photos and find more easily the next time.

HTrend Database

HTrend is the dedicated Hashtrend's database. Conceived in PostgreSQL and imagined to offer you the best User Experience, it will safely stock your photos and personal data.

Hashtrend Services

Strong communication with third-part APIs

Hashtrend communicates with Instagram's "Hashtag Search" API, Flickr's community-supported API and Pinterest's RESTful API through OAUTH safe protocol in order to collect the most pertinents results based on the user's reseach.

Beautiful UI and intuitive UX

Choosing Hashtrend, you can count on a beutiful working and entertainment experience thanks to its modern design and user-friendly graphic interface.

Great community support

Thanks to its interaction with the major photo-based social networks, you can rely on a strong community of users who are able to answer to your questions, no matter their level of complexity. Take a look at our "FAQ" section !

Totally free

Hashtrend's services are totally free to all its levels: from the basic experience with no user's enregistration to the higher database interaction you will not deal with any type of paying subscription. Have fun wit us for free, forever !

Continuous improvements

Hashtrend is regularly updated to always offer you the best performances and stability. Users who want to contribute tho its development can easily do a pull request through this GitHub link

Security first

Your security is our first priority. That's why Hashtrend protects your personal data against phishing, pretexting, ID theft and similar through efficient methods like strong passwords encription, csrf tokens and others. You can safely store your photos and data in HTrend database.
